Early morning loft render

A simple render of an early morning scene I created in Blender.

I modelled the scene roughly following the reference image.
Then, textured it with either textures I found online or made them myself with shaders. (like the glass)
Then, used light groups to render the lights separately and then combined them in the compositor.
Then finally, I used Photoshop to edit the image a little bit.

I used a reference image, but I'm not sure if this is the correct source.
